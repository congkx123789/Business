# On-Call Rotation

## Schedule (example)
- Week-long shifts, handoff Tuesdays 10:00 local
- Primary: Eng A → Eng B → Eng C → Eng D (repeat)
- Secondary mirrors Primary, offset by 1 week

## Calendar
- Maintain shared calendar "SelfCar On-Call" and invite the team
- Include on-call guide, runbook links, and escalation chain in event description

## Contacts
- PagerDuty service: SelfCar-Core
- Slack channel: #incidents
- Phone tree: in PagerDuty escalation policy

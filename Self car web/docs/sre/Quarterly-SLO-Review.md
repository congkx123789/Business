# Quarterly SLO Review (Template)

## Purpose
Ensure SLOs reflect current risk/impact, calibrate targets, and close the loop between signals, incidents, and engineering work.

## Inputs
- Last quarter’s SLO attainment and error budget burn (per SLO)
- Incident summaries and postmortems (SEV1/SEV2)
- Roadmap changes (features, infra, dependencies)
- Business priorities (payments, search, VIN consistency)

## Review Checklist
- Are SLIs still the best proxies for user experience?
- Do SLO targets match business risk (payments highest, VIN consistency strict)?
- Were alerts actionable (low noise, high precision)?
- Error budget policy followed (freezes when exhausted)?
- MTTD/MTTR trends improving?
- Any chronic contributors (top 3) and committed CPAs?

## Decisions & Changes
- SLO target adjustments (if any) and rationale
- Alert threshold/tuning changes
- Reliability investments added to roadmap (owners/dates)

# Runbook Coverage Goals

## Target
- 100% coverage for page-worthy alerts (SEV1/SEV2)
- 90% coverage for recurring SEV3 operational tasks

## Minimum Contents (each runbook)
- Symptoms & dashboards
- Quick triage (5 min)
- Deep dive queries/traces
- Mitigations/rollbacks
- Escalation contacts

## Tracking
- Maintain a checklist per service (payments, api, search, inventory)
- Gate new alerts on having an attached runbook link

## Quarterly Audit
- Sample 5 alerts per service → verify runbook is current and actionable
- Identify gaps; assign owners and dates
